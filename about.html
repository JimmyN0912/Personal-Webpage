Hello, I'm Jim!

I'm a passionate developer and creator based in Taiwan. Although I haven't even got into college yet, my passion and interest in programming drove me to self-learn a lot online.

My journey in technology began when I was still very young, from a book that teaches HTML. Even though I didn't follow the book to actually build a webpage back then, the book introduced me into the world of programming, which I continued to be passionate. Since then, I've worked on numerous projects ranging from simple Python scripts to automations that made not only my life but also people around me easier.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, reading or watching about events in this industry, or enjoying my other passion of aviation.
